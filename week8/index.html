Interactive simulation

My idea was to make a rain that will help the "flower" to glow.
The rain follows the mouse.
As soon as it touches the circle ('flower') it starts frowing. When it's full-it becomes green.
When it's not raining, the flower becomes smaller.

Step 2

Add a circle that represents a flower. I put it in the right down corner. what I kept in mind is the final diametr, cause it will grow.

Step 3

Now I want to make my rain to folow the mouse. In order to do that, I split up the screen into right and left sections (+gravity, to make it go faster).

Step 4

Now I want to change the radius of my circle. I set up the min and max numbers. As soon as the mouse in the right, the radius start growing. When it on the left side, it's going down.
